Agustín Alabarces
Programmer, game designer, librarian, musician. Lives in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Student of Videogame Programming at Hurlingham National University. Sax player and composer of original music for videogames.
Fulbacho

Fulbacho is a board game based on the argentinian football (soccer) league and classic Monopoly style board games. Instead of property you buy players to improve your team, which you use to play matches in a mix of deck-building and dice rolling. Originally created in paper, this Tabletopia version is from 2020. Play now
Turtle vs Rabbit

Turtle vs Rabbit is a game created by Odyssey Studio. It was a project for Introduction to Videogames at university. It's a platformer where the player controls the turtle and tries to beat the hare from the famous fable, using cunning to replace speed. Play now
Horror Light

Horror Light is a videogame created by Odyssey Studio for Conceptual Videogame Design at university. It's a "survival horror" game with isometric view where the protagonist must avoid several monsters while unravelling a gruesome mistery. Play now
Gaucho World Cup

Gaucho World Cup is a videogame created by Odyssey Studio for GameJam+ 2023. It's a minigame compilation based on classic gaucho and argentinian national games, such as the toad, horse taming and "cuadreras" races. Play now
